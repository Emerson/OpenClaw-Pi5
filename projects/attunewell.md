# AttuneWell.app

## What It Is
A web app to help couples practice healthy communication. Couples record themselves during an event (typically a conflict), get a transcript with basic sentiment analysis, and receive feedback from an LLM-powered therapist.

## Core Features
- **Recording** — couple records themselves during conflict/conversation
- **Transcript** — auto-generated with sentiment analysis
- **LLM Therapist** — neutral, critical, grounded insight on the dynamics at play
- **Therapist Lenses** (user-selectable):
  - Gottman
  - EFT (Emotionally Focused Therapy)
  - Attachment
  - CBT
  - General
- **Interactive Transcript** — sentences highlight as audio plays; users can navigate by sentence
- **Block-level Q&A** — highlight a section, ask the therapist about it ("in this block I felt X, what do you think?")
- **General Chat** — open-ended conversation with the LLM therapist per "reflection" session
