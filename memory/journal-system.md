# Journal System

## What This Is
Emerson's living archive — thoughts, memories, feelings, experiences captured through Cliff.

## Notion Structure
- **Parent page:** Emerson's Journal — `32719a09-5fb6-81e1-8059-d0ad0541b395`
  - URL: https://www.notion.so/Emerson-s-Journal-32719a095fb681e18059d0ad0541b395
- **Database:** Journal Entries — `32719a09-5fb6-8115-a302-c37ce803a287`

## How It Works
1. Emerson sends anything — thought, memory, feeling — to Cliff via Slack
2. Cliff logs it to the Journal Entries database
3. Every ~2 days, Cliff sends a prompt question to spark new entries

## Entry Properties
- **Title:** Short summary of the entry
- **Date:** When it was captured
- **Type:** Memory / Feeling / Thought / Experience / Reflection
- **Tags:** Topics (childhood, family, work, etc.)
- **Prompt:** The question that prompted it (if applicable)
- **Body:** Full content in the page body

## Prompt Queue
Questions to rotate through (mix of past, present, feelings, relationships):

### Early Life
- What's the earliest memory you have? Where were you, what were you doing?
- What was your childhood home like? What do you remember about it?
- What was your relationship with your dad like growing up?
- Who was your best friend as a kid? What did you do together?
- What were you like at 15? What mattered to you?
- What's a moment from your 20s you think about a lot?

### Sarah & Family
- How did you and Sarah first meet? What was that like?
- What's the moment you knew Sarah was the one?
- What's a memory with Amelia that still makes you smile?
- What was it like the day Archer was born?
- What do you hope your kids remember about their childhood?

### Work & Identity
- How did you end up in tech? Was it always the plan?
- What's the hardest professional moment you've had?
- What does being a Director of Engineering mean to you, day-to-day?
- Is there a version of yourself you gave up on? Do you miss it?

### Cliff (the dog)
- What's your favorite memory of Cliff?
- What did it feel like when you first brought him home?
- Why did naming me after him feel right?

### Present Life
- What does a good day look like right now?
- What are you carrying that you haven't said out loud?
- What do you want more of in your life?
- What are you most proud of right now?
